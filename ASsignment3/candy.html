About Kismi Candy

Kismi Candy is a popular candy brand in India known for its unique taste and long history. Here are some interesting facts about Kismi Candy:

  • Kismi Candy was first introduced in 1971 by Parle Products in Mumbai, India.
  • The candy is made with jaggery, a traditional Indian sweetener made from sugarcane juice.
  • Kismi Candy is a chewy toffee candy that comes in different flavors, including strawberry, orange, and mango.
  • The candy is popular among people of all ages in India, and is often sold at small kiosks and stores across the country.
